Before you buy

Common questions, answered straight.

How is the dose actually defined?

Our engineering target is FiH₂ — the percentage of hydrogen in the air you inhale. The closed-loop reservoir, one-way valve, and electrolysis cell are all sized to maintain a defined FiH₂ within the clinically studied, non-flammable range, regardless of breathing rate.

What if it doesn't work for me?

60-day risk-free trial with free return shipping. If it's not part of your routine after two months, send it back, full refund. No restocking fees.

How loud is it?

Under 38 dB — quieter than a refrigerator. Most customers use it during meditation, reading, or work.

Can I use it while pregnant or with a medical condition?

Always consult your physician first. Hydrogen has a strong safety profile in published research, but InhaleH₂ is not a medical treatment and is not intended for any specific condition.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently US, Canada, UK, EU, Australia, and Japan. Other regions case-by-case — contact our team.
